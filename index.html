CodeFocus
Phone time while your AI codes. Locks when it stops.

CodeFocus locks distracting apps on your iPhone — TikTok, Instagram, whatever eats your day — and only unlocks them while an AI coding agent (like Claude Code) is actually running on your Mac. It stops, your apps lock. No willpower. Pure leverage.

Set up your Mac

Paste this once in your Mac's Terminal — it connects Claude Code to CodeFocus over Bluetooth:

curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/codefocusapp/setup/main/install.sh | bash

Requires a Mac  running Claude Code + Bluetooth on both devices.
